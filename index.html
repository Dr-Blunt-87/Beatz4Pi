1. Overview

Beatz4Pi is a music exchange community on Pi Network. By using it you agree to these Terms and Pi Network developer policies.

2. Eligibility

  • Must be a verified Pi Network Pioneer.
  • Must be 18+ or have guardian consent.
  • One account per Pioneer.

3. Pi Payments

All transactions use Pi via Pi Network official payment API. We never store Pi or your wallet keys.

4. External Links

You may share YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Instagram and Facebook links. We are not responsible for third-party content.

5. Subscription

Pioneer Pro costs 1 Pi per month (1 Test Pi on Testnet). Payment goes to the Beatz4Pi app wallet. Non-refundable on Mainnet.

6. File Transfers

PM transfers limited to 64MB per message.

7. Prohibited Content

Spam, hate speech, profanity, adult content and non-music content result in immediate suspension.

8. Termination

We may suspend accounts violating these Terms or Pi Network policies.

1. Data We Collect

Only what is needed: your Pi username, posts, links and messages.

2. Authentication

Handled by Pi Network. We receive only your username and UID, never your seed phrase or private keys.

3. Storage

Browser local storage only for session and preferences. No advertising cookies or third-party trackers.

4. Messages

Messages encrypted in transit. Files not permanently stored beyond delivery.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Retention

Data retained while account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

1. Your Content

You retain full copyright to beats and tracks you share. Posting does not transfer ownership.

2. License to Display

By posting you grant Beatz4Pi a limited, non-exclusive license to display your post in the platform only.

3. External Links

When sharing links you confirm you are the rights holder or have permission to share that content.

4. Pi Licensing

Beat licensing via Pi is a direct agreement between Pioneers. Keep Pi transaction records as proof.

5. No Unauthorized Sampling

Do not post content containing samples you do not have rights to use.

6. File Transfer Responsibility

Any files sent via PM are the sole responsibility of the sender. Beatz4Pi does not monitor, store or take responsibility for files transferred between Pioneers. All file transfers must comply with copyright law and Pi Network guidelines.

6. Takedowns

Report infringement with your name and the infringing URL. We act within 72 hours.

7. Repeat Infringers

Accounts with repeated violations permanently suspended per Pi Network guidelines.

8. File Transfers

File transfers via PM are temporary and stored only in the browser session — Beatz4Pi does not store any files on servers. Pioneers are solely responsible for ensuring they have full rights to share any files, beats, samples or recordings sent via the platform. Sharing copyrighted material without permission is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account suspension.

1. Be a Real Pioneer

Treat every Pioneer with respect, dignity and professionalism.

2. Music Only

All posts must be music or beat related. Spam removed immediately.

3. No Fake Previews

Links must lead to genuine music content. Misleading links result in permanent suspension.

4. Language

Profanity, hate speech, discrimination and harassment are strictly prohibited. Auto-filter is active.

5. Fair Pi Dealing

All Pi transactions must be honest. Scams result in account termination and reporting to Pi Network.

6. Respect IP

Only share music you have rights to. Do not steal beats or claim others work as your own.

7. Pi Network Rules

All Pi Network platform policies and KYC requirements apply in full within Beatz4Pi at all times.
