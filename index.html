Beatz4Pi is a Pi Network music community. By registering you accept these Terms, Pi Network Developer Policies, and all Pi Network community guidelines in full.

2. Eligibility

  • Must be a KYC-verified Pi Network Pioneer.
  • Must be 18+ or have guardian consent.
  • One account per Pioneer. Multiple accounts result in permanent ban.

3. User Responsibility

You are solely responsible for all content you post, share or send via Beatz4Pi including posts, messages, links and files. Beatz4Pi and its creator disclaim all liability for user-generated content.

4. Messages & Chats

Beatz4Pi does not store or retain messages as evidence on your behalf. If you are involved in a copyright dispute, transaction disagreement or any legal matter, it is your own responsibility as a Pioneer to save and document relevant conversations before deleting them. Beatz4Pi and its creator accept no liability for lost messages or failure to retain evidence.

5. Pi Payments

All Pi transactions use the official Pi Network payment API. We never store Pi or wallet keys. All Pi transactions are final and non-refundable.

6. External Links

You may share YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Instagram and Facebook links only. You confirm you have rights to share linked content.

7. Subscription

Pioneer Pro costs 1 Pi/month. Non-refundable on Mainnet.

8. Prohibited Content

Spam, hate speech, profanity, scams, adult content and off-topic content result in immediate suspension and reporting to Pi Network.

9. Copyright Disclaimer

Beatz4Pi is not responsible for copyright infringement by users. All copyright claims are the sole responsibility of the posting Pioneer.

10. File & Link Sharing via DM

Pioneers may share links to music files via DM. Use services like WeTransfer, Google Drive or Dropbox to share files and paste the link in chat. Only share files you own or have rights to distribute. Beatz4Pi does not host or store any files.

11. Termination

We may suspend or permanently ban accounts violating these Terms or Pi Network policies without notice.

1. Data We Collect

Only what is needed: your Pi username, posts, links and messages.

2. Authentication

Handled by Pi Network. We receive only your username and UID, never your seed phrase or private keys.

3. Storage

Browser local storage only for session and preferences. No advertising cookies or third-party trackers.

4. Messages

Messages encrypted in transit. Files not permanently stored beyond delivery.

5. File & Link Sharing

Beatz4Pi does not host or store any files. File sharing is done via external links (WeTransfer, Google Drive etc.) pasted in DM. Beatz4Pi does not access or log any files shared between users.

7. Retention

Data retained while account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
